About JDS Ceilings & Partitions

JDS Ceilings & Partitions is a small, hands-on business based in Newcastle upon Tyne. I’m Jason, and I’ve spent over 10 years on the tools working on ceilings, partitions, media walls and drylining across homes, shops and commercial buildings.

I look after the job from start to finish – from the first message and measuring up, through to doing the work and making sure everything is left clean and tidy.

What I specialise in

  • MF ceilings and suspended grid ceilings
  • Plasterboard ceilings, bulkheads and feature details
  • Metal stud walls and room splits
  • Media walls & TV feature walls
  • Soundproofing and insulation upgrades
  • Ceiling & wall repairs for leaks and damage

Why customers choose JDS

  • Honest advice – I’ll tell you what will work best, not just the most expensive option.
  • Reliable – I turn up when I say I will and keep you updated.
  • Good finish – straight lines, solid fixing and tidy work.
  • Respect for your home – dust sheets down, rubbish cleared, no mess left behind.

Areas covered

I mainly work across Newcastle upon Tyne and nearby areas around the North East. If you’re not sure if you’re in range, just send a quick message and I’ll let you know.
